The Dhakkan Files

This page was supposed to be something else.

Then you read the vault for the third time. Then you said, “Acha ni hota to 3 baar ku padhti.” And then, of course, you called me Dhakkan.

So today, this Dhakkan is keeping a small record of things that are funny, soft, and a little difficult to say properly.
Case file 1: The third read

Proof collected: Golu Babu read it three times. Case closed. This Dhakkan will not ask again whether you liked it.

Case file 2: The ordinary day

The work-from-home day was never really about work. It was about one ordinary day together. Laptops, calls, small breaks, random teasing, and just being in the same space for a few hours.

Before your life changes, Kanika — and I genuinely wish from my heart that it changes in all the ways you want and deserve — things will also change between us. That is why that one simple day mattered to me more than I was able to explain.

Case file 3: The line that hurt

You said you want to do things for me, but even if you want to, you cannot.

It hurt. Not because I doubted you. It hurt because I understood you.

Sometimes the most painful thing is not that someone does not care. It is knowing that they care, and still some things are not possible.

Case file 4: The shorter truth

This means a lot, Golu Babu ❤️

I made all of this thinking only about you. So if you’re liking it, even a little, then all the effort feels worth it.

Bas aaram se padhna… har page mein thoda sa main hoon, thoda sa tum ho, aur thoda sa hum.

A Small Dhakkan Quiz

Choose first what you think my mind has to say. No wrong answer. All option answers are the same for that question, because the truth underneath is one. Just choose whatever comes first in your mind.

1. When I kept asking for one work-from-home day, what do you think I really wanted?
It was never about work. It was about one normal day with you. Nothing extraordinary. Just one ordinary memory before your life changes in the ways I genuinely wish for you.
2. When I taunt you about time, what do you think my mind is actually trying to say?
The real answer is: I miss you. Sometimes I say it badly. Sometimes it comes out as taunts. That is my fault. Underneath all of it, there is usually one simple thing — I miss you, and I still don’t always know how to say that without sounding hurt.
3. When you said, “Even if I want to, I can’t do anything for you,” why did it hurt?
It hurt because I understood you. I know you care. I know you want to do things. And I also know some realities are bigger than both of us. That is what made it hurt quietly.

Something From Your Heart

From the bottom of my heart...

I wish the best for you, Kanika. Truly. Whatever you want in life, whatever you are hoping for, whatever will make your life fuller and happier — I wish that for you with all my heart.

And I also know that when your life changes, things between us will change too. Maybe that is why some small moments feel so big to me right now.

I care for you, Golu Babu. You will always be my Golu Babu. I have a lot of love for you — maybe more than I was able to say properly today.

When you asked me how much I am into you, I gave a very wrong answer. Maybe I said it lightly because I got scared of how honest the real answer is. But I think you understood.

There is a lot of love, Golu Babu. I don’t know if you fully understand it or not, but there is a lot. And this page is not asking you to fix that, return that, or carry any pressure. It is just me saying it honestly and gently.
