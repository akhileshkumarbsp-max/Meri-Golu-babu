Day 4

The Ways We Took Care Of Each Other

Maybe the most beautiful thing about us was not how much we felt.

Maybe it was how naturally we cared.

Golu Babu,

I don't think this was ever only about big moments. It was always the small things.

The way you noticed my mood. The way you wanted to cheer me up. The way your good morning messages became a part of my day. The way even after fights, neither of us could stay away for too long.

And somewhere in all these small things, care quietly became our language.

A few lines I want to keep here

The way u make me eat in lunch and jab bhi fruits lata tha meko khilata tha phke
Bht sara pyaar from me babu
Tight wali jhappi🤗🤗🤗😘
U know we can't be upset with each other for more time

These lines will always stay with me.

Not because they were perfectly written, but because they carried us in the most honest way — care, comfort, love, and that strange softness where even after a fight, we both knew we could not stay upset for too long.

A conversation I never forgot

One day I asked you:

Don't you know how much I love you? How much I care about you?

How close I feel to you? How important you are to me? How much I miss you? How I look at you? How special I try to make you feel? How much I am with you in every situation? How much good I want for you? How much support I try to give you? How how how much...

And you replied:

I know very well how much u care for me.
I can see in your eyes.

I don't think you realise how long that stayed with me.

Because sometimes we spend so much time trying to express what we feel, and then one day we realise the person already knew.

The rose was never the important part

There is a memory that always makes me smile.

Not because of the flower itself.

But because you gave it to me, and then that moment stayed between us like a small memory.

Flowers dry. People forget small gestures. But some moments stay because of the person who gave them.

The rose was never important. The fact that it became our memory was.
