Day 6

What Changed In Me

You brought back something I thought I had lost.

For a long time, I had started believing that maybe nobody would really understand me. Maybe I had to carry things alone. Maybe care, softness, and emotional safety were things other people experienced, not me.

Then you came into my life.

Not with big promises. Not with dramatic words. But through small things.

A good morning message. A random call. A voice note. A hug. The way you understood my mood. The way you could see the child in me when most people only saw anger or toughness.

You made me feel understood. That is not a small gift. It is the rarest one.

You made me imagine that if I ever build a life with someone, I would want qualities that you have: care, patience, emotional safety, kindness, and love that shows up in small actions.

You did not ask me to change. You just made me want to be someone who deserved the care you were giving.

You brought happiness back into parts of my life that had become very quiet.

And for that, Kanika Babu, I will always be grateful.

Tell me one thing from your heart today.
