Day 7

Things Left Unsaid

There are feelings that exist between two people even when life does not give those feelings an easy place to stand.

I do not want to pretend that nothing exists. That would be unfair to what we have shared.

At the same time, I know your reality. I know there are responsibilities, boundaries, timing, and chapters that were already written before we became this close.

So this page is not here to ask anything from you. It is here only to say the truth gently.

Some bonds do not ask for a name. They simply ask to be remembered honestly.

I care for you deeply. I respect you deeply. And yes, a part of me will always wish life had given us more room.

More ordinary conversations. More peaceful walks. More time where we did not have to think about the clock. More moments where I could simply be there for you.

But even if a story cannot become everything the heart imagines, it can still remain beautiful.

And what we have shared will always remain beautiful to me.

If there is one thing you want me to know, write it here.
